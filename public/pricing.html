Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing accordingly.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay by invoice.

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

Yes! Both Pro and Enterprise plans include commercial usage rights. You can use AuroraX for unlimited client projects.
